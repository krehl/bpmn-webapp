What is BPMN

BPMN is a standard Business Process Model and Notation, which provides businesses with the capability of understanding their internal business procedures in a graphical notation and gives organizations the ability to communicate these procedures in a standard manner. Furthermore, the graphical notation facilitates the understanding of the performance collaborations and business transactions between the organizations. This ensures that businesses will understand themselves and participants in their business and will enable organizations to adjust to new internal and B2B business circumstances quickly.

About Process Modeller

Process Modeller is a free-to-use webapp to visualize BPMN diagramms. This webapp uses the BPMN.io framework as it's core.

The Repository

In the Repository you can view and access your stored BPMN project, displayed on project cards. Your options are to open, export and delete the BPMN project file.

History & version controll

This feature, only available trough the modeller page, allows you to view the changes you did to your project and if necessary, revert to an older state.

Import a BPM project

Here you can import a BPMN project file to your Process Modeller workspace.

Export a diagramm

The process Modeller allows you to export your BPMN project as project XML file, a SVG or PNG picture.
